About FoodLink

FoodLink is a community-driven initiative by Impact Makers aimed at reducing food waste and bridging the gap between surplus and scarcity. We connect mess owners, restaurants, and other food providers with orphanages, old-age homes, and institutions in need, ensuring excess food reaches those who need it most.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a sustainable system that minimizes food waste while providing nourishment to vulnerable groups. By fostering collaboration between donors and recipients, we aim to build a network of kindness, care, and resilience.

How It Works

  1. Sign Up: Mess owners, restaurants, and institutions register on our platform.
  2. Donate: Food donors list their surplus food available for donation.
  3. Connect: Recipients browse listings and request food based on their needs.
  4. Deliver: A seamless system ensures timely delivery to the designated locations.

Our Impact

Since our inception, FoodLink has successfully redistributed thousands of meals, reducing food waste while supporting vulnerable communities. Together, we can make a greater impact.
